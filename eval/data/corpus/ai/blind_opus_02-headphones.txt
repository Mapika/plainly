# AuraPods Pro

Silence, redesigned.

The AuraPods Pro don't just block out the world; they hand you a private one. Our third-generation adaptive noise cancellation reads your surroundings up to 50,000 times a second, melting away the rumble of a subway car, the drone of an open office, and the hum of a long-haul flight before it ever reaches your ears. Flip to Transparency mode with a tap, and the room rushes back in, crisp and natural, so you never miss a boarding call or a barista's question.

Sound is where the AuraPods Pro truly sing. Custom 11mm drivers deliver deep, controlled bass and vocals so clear they feel like they're in the room with you. Spatial audio with head tracking turns any playlist or film into a three-dimensional experience that moves as you do.

Comfort was engineered, not assumed. Featherlight ear cushions and a balanced headband let you wear them through back-to-back meetings or an entire transatlantic flight without fatigue.

With 40 hours of battery on a single charge, a five-minute quick top-up for four extra hours, and seamless switching between all your devices, the AuraPods Pro keep up with everything you do.

Hear everything. Or nothing at all. Your call.
