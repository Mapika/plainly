# AuraPods Pro — Wireless Noise-Cancelling Headphones

Some headphones play music. The AuraPods Pro disappear into it.

Engineered for listeners who refuse to compromise, the AuraPods Pro combine adaptive noise cancellation with audiophile-grade sound in a design light enough to forget you're wearing them. Three layers of acoustic isolation — including our proprietary HushField technology — eliminate up to 98% of ambient noise, whether you're on a flight, at a desk, or in a city that won't quiet down.

The sound is precise and warm: 40mm custom drivers tuned to deliver deep, articulate bass without muddying the mids, and highs that shimmer without fatigue. EQ presets are available in the companion app, but the default tuning is good enough that most listeners never touch them.

Battery life runs to 36 hours on a full charge, with a ten-minute quick charge giving you three additional hours when you're running late. Multipoint pairing lets you stay connected to two devices simultaneously — switch from laptop to phone without lifting a finger.

The over-ear fit uses memory foam cushions wrapped in breathable protein leather, contoured to seal comfortably across a full range of head shapes. Foldable hinges and a slim carry case make them genuinely portable.

The AuraPods Pro are not the cheapest option. They are, however, the one you will reach for every day.
