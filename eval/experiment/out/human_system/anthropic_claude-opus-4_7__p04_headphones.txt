**AuraPods Pro**

The AuraPods Pro are for people who want quiet on demand. Flip the switch on the left cup and the office HVAC, the espresso grinder, the guy on the phone two rows back — they all fade out. What's left is your music, or nothing at all, which is sometimes the point.

Battery runs 38 hours with noise cancelling on, closer to 50 without. A ten-minute charge gets you five hours, so you can panic less before a flight. The drivers are 40mm, tuned warm but not muddy, and the bass holds up at low volumes (a small thing most headphones get wrong).

The earcups are memory foam wrapped in protein leather. They breathe better than the previous model. Bluetooth 5.3, multipoint pairing across two devices, USB-C, a 3.5mm jack for the stubborn.

Available in matte black, sand, and a deep forest green. $349.
