## Beyond the Screen: Why You Need More Books in Your Life

In a world saturated with digital distractions, it's easy to let books gather dust. But putting down your phone and picking up a book offers real benefits that screens can't replicate.

Reading **expands your mind**. Each page introduces new ideas, cultures, and perspectives, building empathy and sharper thinking. It also improves vocabulary, memory, and focus in ways that carry over into daily life.

Books are also a powerful **stress reliever**. Losing yourself in a compelling narrative offers an escape from daily pressures, and unlike endless scrolling, reading gives you a sustained, immersive experience that actually lets you unwind — and sleep better afterward.

So the next time you reach for your phone to kill a few minutes, try a book instead. You might be surprised where it takes you.
