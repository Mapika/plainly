AuraPods Pro Wireless Noise-Cancelling Headphones

The AuraPods Pro are built around hybrid active noise cancellation that blocks up to 95% of external sound. Paired with 40mm graphene drivers, they produce balanced audio with solid bass and clear highs. Bluetooth 5.2 keeps the connection stable out to 30 feet.

The memory foam ear cushions and lightweight frame hold up through long listening sessions without discomfort. Battery life runs 25 hours per charge, and a 10-minute quick charge adds another 3 hours when you're short on time.

Available in matte black and silver.
