The AuraPods Pro are wireless noise-cancelling headphones built around Active Noise Cancellation technology tuned to cut ambient sound and keep you in your music. The audio is detailed and bass-heavy without sacrificing clarity. Battery life runs to 24 hours, and a 15-minute charge adds another 5 hours when you're running low.

The frame is foldable and built from durable materials, so they hold up in a bag without taking up much space. The ergonomic fit keeps them comfortable over long sessions. Bluetooth 5.0 handles device pairing, and the built-in microphone picks up your voice clearly on calls.
