The AuraPods Pro are wireless noise-cancelling headphones built for serious listeners. Advanced noise-cancelling technology blocks out background noise so you can focus on your music, podcast, or call without distraction. The ergonomic design holds up through long sessions — commuting, working, or just unwinding at home.

A touch control interface lets you manage playback and calls without reaching for your phone. Battery life runs up to 30 hours on a single charge, and if you're running low, 15 minutes of fast charging buys you another 3 hours. They work with both iOS and Android and come with a compact carrying case.

Clean sound, all-day comfort, and a battery that keeps pace with your schedule — that's the AuraPods Pro.
