## Don't Lose a Moment: Safely Back Up Your Photos!

Losing precious photos is a nightmare. Here's how to keep them safe.

**1. The "Rule of Three":** Keep at least **three copies** of your photos — the original plus two backups stored separately.

**2. Local Backup:**
* **External Hard Drive:** Buy a reliable external hard drive, connect it to your computer, and drag your photo folders onto it weekly or monthly. Label it clearly.
* **Computer Hard Drive:** Your computer holds the originals, but it's not a backup. If it dies, those photos go with it.

**3. Cloud Backup:**
* **Choose a Service:** Google Photos, iCloud, Dropbox, and OneDrive all offer free storage tiers with paid plans for larger libraries.
* **Automatic Sync:** Most cloud services sync automatically from your phone or computer — turn this on and leave it running.
* **Check Your Uploads:** Log in occasionally to confirm photos are actually uploading. Don't assume.

**4. Review and Organize:** Before backing up, delete blurry shots and duplicates, then sort photos into labeled folders ("2023 Vacations," "Family Events"). You'll thank yourself the next time you need to find something.

Follow these steps and you'll have a solid backup system that won't let you down.
