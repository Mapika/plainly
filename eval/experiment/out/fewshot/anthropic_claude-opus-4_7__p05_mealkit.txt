**FreshCrate: Dinner, Without the Drama**

Look, we all know how it goes. It's 6 p.m., the fridge contains half an onion and some questionable yogurt, and someone is asking what's for dinner in that particular tone. So you order pizza. Again.

FreshCrate is for people who want to cook but don't want to plan, shop, chop, or stand in a grocery aisle wondering what shallots actually look like. Each week, we send you a box of real ingredients — the good stuff, measured out and ready — along with recipes a reasonably distracted person can actually follow. Most meals come together in about thirty minutes. Some are fancy. Some are the kind of thing you'd eat in pajamas. All of them taste like you meant to make them.

Skip a week whenever. Cancel whenever. No guilt, no spreadsheet of unused vegetables wilting in the crisper.

Just dinner. Handled.
